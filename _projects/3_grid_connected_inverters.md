The efficient integration of solar power into the main electrical grid is crucial for modern energy systems. This project explores the advances in topologies and control techniques for grid-connected inverters used in photovoltaic (PV) energy harvesting.

The research investigates the performance, efficiency, and power quality of various inverter designs under different operating conditions. Emphasis is placed on developing sophisticated control strategies that enhance grid stability, manage active and reactive power flows, and improve overall system reliability.

### Project Focus Areas

- Review and analysis of cutting-edge grid-connected inverter topologies.
- Development of advanced control techniques for enhanced power quality.
- Strategies for improving the stability and efficiency of PV energy harvesting systems.
